Safety on the beach

While we do our best here are Topsail to provide the most comfortable and safe beach experience for you, there are things you should keep in mind before heading out to the ocean!

1. Check the Weather!

You should always check the weather before you head out to the beach. Wind can make waves unsafe to play in. There is also the risk of hurricanes and other tropical storms potentially tossing sand into your eyes or other objects that may be harmful.

2. Swim in Designated Areas

Make sure that when you enter the water you are within view of our various lifegaurds that we have posted about the beach. This ensures that if you do happen to run into any trouble we are able to see you and assist as soon as possible.

3. Avoid Currents

When swimming, if you can feel yourself getting dragged out towards the open ocean the most important thing to do is to remain calm and swim sideways. This will help you fight the current and get you back to shore. One of the worst things to do is panic.

4. Never Swim Alone

Always make sure you have a buddy with you when you are in the water. It can provide an extra level of safety that can be the difference between a life or death situation.
